The Akina Speed Stars is aamateur racing group, they are known as the weakest team in Gunma. Their leader, "Koichiro Iketani" is one one of the friend of Takumi Fujiwara. They are principaly active at the Akina Pass. Takumi is not an official member of the Akina Speed Stars, but he sometimes do races for them, but that's all.
