Informations

Initial D (頭文字D, Inisharu Dī?) is a seinen manga, by Shūichi Shigeno. It was published between june 1995 and july 2013 in the "Young Magazine" magazine. It has 48 tomes and had an anime with multiples seasons, oav, and films. There is some video games too. The story is focused in racing cars where the main caracter uses a not very good one.

Something that marked a generation, is the music gender that we can hear in the animated show, its called "Eurobeat" and the Anime take part in the popularisation of it. Here an exemple : "Leslie Perrish - Killing my love".
