Bunta Fujiwara is the father of Takumi. He is 43 years old, and was a rally pilot, he quited to make and sell tofu. He's the owner of the AE86. The AE86 is a bit modified, because Bunta has a lot of knowledge about mechanic. its with this vehicle that he's known as the unbeatable man of the Akina Pass. Bunta lives alone with Takumi. Sometimes, he doesn't act like a great father everytime, as the pictures under the text can show...
